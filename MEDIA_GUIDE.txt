FITNESS V3 — EXERCISE MEDIA GUIDE
=================================

CURRENT FORMAT
--------------
• 55 MP4 files in assets/videos/
• 55 WebP posters in assets/posters/
• 360 × 600 portrait video
• approximately 3-second seamless loops
• muted video; coaching audio is supplied by the app
• original light anatomy-style body with red working-muscle highlights

REPLACING A VIDEO LATER
-----------------------
Keep exactly the same file name.
Example:
  assets/videos/squat.mp4
  assets/posters/squat.webp

Recommended upgraded clip specification:
• 720 × 1280 or 1080 × 1920 portrait master
• 6–12 seconds
• H.264 MP4, yuv420p, fast-start enabled
• full body visible
• clean light background
• safe and accurate movement
• highlighted target muscles
• no watermark, username or copied branding
• muted audio
• matching poster image

After replacement, change the cache version in sw.js so installed users receive the new files.
