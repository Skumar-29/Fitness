Balanced fitness

🔥Reduce fat

Train consistently, walk regularly, manage portions and avoid extreme crash diets. Fat loss occurs across the body, not from one exercise.

🫁Build stamina

Use the easier version first, keep a sustainable pace and gradually spend more time at moderate intensity.

💪Shape the body

Strengthen every major muscle group, use controlled technique and progress resistance or difficulty over time.

🌙Recover

Use Day 7 for light activity and aim for regular, good-quality sleep so training can be repeated safely.

Vegetarian nutrition checklist

Protein

Lentils, beans, tofu, tempeh, dairy, eggs, soy milk, nuts and seeds.

Iron

Legumes, tofu, leafy greens and fortified foods. Add vitamin-C-rich food to meals.

Vitamin B12

Use reliable fortified foods or discuss supplementation with a qualified clinician, especially for vegan diets.

Calcium + iodine

Choose calcium-fortified alternatives and check labels; use iodised salt in modest amounts unless medically restricted.

Portions are flexible

This plan is a balanced template, not a prescribed calorie diet. Adjust portions for hunger, body size, workload and medical needs.

Safety screening

Seek medical advice before starting if you have a heart or lung condition, uncontrolled blood pressure, recent surgery, unexplained fainting, pregnancy-related restrictions, or an injury that changes how you move.

Evidence and guidance

This app follows current Australian adult movement guidance: activity on most days, muscle strengthening at least twice weekly, functional activity on at least three days, less prolonged sitting and adequate sleep.

Meal guidance follows the Australian healthy eating framework and highlights vegetarian protein, iron and vitamin B12 considerations.
