REPLACEMENT STANDARD
--------------------
Recommended delivery for each future clip:
• H.264 MP4
• yuv420p pixel format
• fast-start enabled
• 720 × 1280 portrait or an exercise-appropriate high-resolution layout
• normally 6–12 seconds
• 24 or 30 fps
• no audio
• seamless or visually smooth loop
• matching WebP poster

Use the stable exercise ID as the filename. Example:
  assets/videos/pushup.mp4
  assets/posters/pushup.webp

Before approving a clip, check:
• correct exercise and target muscles
• accurate joint alignment and safe range
• natural hands, feet and limb proportions
• no body distortion between frames
• no visible motion jump at the loop point
• body is large enough to learn from on a phone
• movement is understandable without audio
• correct front/side angle

After adding an approved file:
1. Add the exercise ID to APPROVED_MEDIA in app.js.
2. Add the poster to the service-worker shell only when it should always be available offline.
3. Increase the cache version in app.js and sw.js.
4. Test normal, Focus Mode, fullscreen, portrait and landscape playback.
5. Test the English and Hindi narration timing.
