FITNESS V4 — ENGLISH/HINDI LANGUAGE & VOICE GUIDE
=================================================

LANGUAGE
--------
Use the EN/हि button for instant switching, or open:
Settings → App language → English / हिन्दी

The selected language applies to:
• navigation and settings
• exercise names and written guidance
• target-muscle labels
• benefits and common mistakes
• meal guidance
• spoken coaching, countdowns and rest/next-exercise announcements

The choice is stored locally and can also sync to signed-in devices after Firebase is connected.

VOICE MODES
-----------
Off
  No spoken coaching.

Essential cues
  Exercise name, countdown and short form/safety reminders.

Full instruction + cues
  Complete setup guidance before the exercise plus selected coaching cues during the interval.

CONTROLS
--------
• Voice preference: automatic, female preferred or male preferred where available.
• Speaking speed: slow, normal or fast.
• Spoken countdown: on/off.
• Form reminders: on/off.
• On-screen subtitles: on/off.
• Repeat instruction: available during workouts.
• Stop voice: immediately cancels queued speech.
• Pausing, skipping or leaving a workout cancels old speech so it does not overlap the next exercise.

AUTOMATIC FLOW
--------------
When enabled:
exercise → rest → spoken next-exercise preview → 3, 2, 1 → next exercise

The app speaks in the currently selected language. Full instructions may be used for the first round, while short cues keep later rounds from becoming overloaded.

HINDI VOICE ON IPHONE
---------------------
The app requests a Hindi (India) voice, but the actual voice comes from iOS/Safari. If Hindi is unavailable or pronunciation is poor:

1. Open iPhone Settings.
2. Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices.
3. Select Hindi and download a preferred voice.
4. Reopen Fitness V4.
5. Use the voice preview in Settings.

Hindi text remains available even when a Hindi speech voice is not installed.
