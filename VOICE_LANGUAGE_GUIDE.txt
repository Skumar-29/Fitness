FITNESS V3 — LANGUAGE AND VOICE GUIDE
=====================================

LANGUAGE
--------
Use the EN/हि button in the top-right corner for instant switching, or open:
Settings → App language → English / हिन्दी

The choice is remembered for future launches. Workout history remains unchanged when the language changes.

VOICE MODES
-----------
Off
  No spoken coaching.

Essential cues
  Announces the exercise and short safety/form reminders.

Full instruction + cues
  Speaks the exercise name and complete Hindi or English setup guidance, plus selected timing cues.

OTHER CONTROLS
--------------
• Voice preference: Automatic, female preferred or male preferred.
• Speaking speed: Slow, normal or fast.
• Spoken countdown: On/off.
• Form reminders: On/off.
• On-screen subtitles: On/off.
• Repeat instruction: available during every workout.
• Stop voice: immediately cancels queued speech.

HINDI VOICE ON IPHONE
---------------------
The app requests a Hindi (India) voice, but the actual voice comes from iOS/Safari. If Hindi is missing or pronunciation is poor:

1. Open iPhone Settings.
2. Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices.
3. Select Hindi and download a preferred voice.
4. Reopen Fitness V3.
5. Settings → Preview voice.

The app still shows Hindi text even if a Hindi voice is unavailable.
