FITNESS V6.1 — HIGH KNEES RESPONSIVE PLAYER REVIEW

Build: fitness-v6.1.0-high-knees-responsive-review

Data compatibility: Existing Fitness V4/V5 local progress remains readable through the existing storage record.

PURPOSE OF THIS BUILD

This is the approval build for the exercise-video experience before the remaining exercise media is replaced.

The High Knees screen now includes:
• clean model-only exercise footage with no title, muscle graphic or instruction baked into the video
• full human body visible during the movement
• a crisp separate target-muscle card for core, hip flexors, quadriceps and calves, with glutes shown as secondary
• accurate timed-cardio labels: Work, Rounds and Change
• three clear movement steps
• compact previous, play/pause and next controls
• responsive portrait and landscape layouts using the visible device viewport
• a no-scroll landscape arrangement with steps left, exercise video centre and muscle/timing information right
• compact How, Tips, Breathing and Rest tabs
• English/Hindi coaching and voice compatibility retained
• existing progress, meals, settings, backup and Firebase cloud-sync structure retained

IMPORTANT MEDIA STATUS

Only High Knees has been converted to the approved realistic video format in this review build. Other exercises retain the previous built-in guided animation until the High Knees layout is approved.

PUBLISHING

Upload everything inside this folder to the root of the GitHub Pages repository. Do not upload the ZIP by itself. Keep firebase-config.js if it already contains your working Firebase configuration.

SAFETY

This app provides general educational fitness guidance. It is not medical diagnosis, rehabilitation, physiotherapy or an individually prescribed exercise program. Stop for chest pain, faintness, severe or unusual breathlessness, or sharp/worsening pain, and seek appropriate professional advice where needed.
