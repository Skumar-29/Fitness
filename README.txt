WHAT IS INCLUDED
----------------
• Six structured workout days plus one recovery day.
• Exact 30-, 45- and 60-minute workout versions.
• 55 original anatomy-style MP4 exercise loops and 55 matching WebP posters.
• Clearly highlighted target muscles and full-body movement demonstrations.
• English and Hindi interface switching.
• English and Hindi spoken coaching using the phone/browser speech engine.
• Full instruction, essential-cue and voice-off modes.
• Countdown, halfway and form reminders.
• On-screen spoken subtitles and a repeat-instruction button.
• Vegetarian, Indian vegetarian, vegan and high-protein meal guidance.
• Progress, measurements, fitness tests, backup and restore.
• Offline app shell and optional downloadable video packs.
• Low-data mode, reduced-motion mode and light/dark/system themes.
• Automatic import of compatible Fitness/Health App V2 local data.

IMPORTANT MEDIA NOTE
--------------------
The included videos are newly generated, original anatomy-style vector MP4 loops. They are clearer than the previous dark robotic demonstrations and do not copy the Instagram recording or its watermark. They are not photorealistic filmed humans. The app is media-ready: any exercise MP4 and poster can later be replaced with a professionally produced or licensed anatomical 3D clip without changing workout history or the training engine.

VOICE COACH
-----------
The app uses the device Web Speech voice. English normally uses an Australian English voice when available. Hindi requests a hi-IN voice. Voice names and quality depend on the phone and browser.

On iPhone, if Hindi pronunciation is not good, install a Hindi system voice in:
Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → Hindi
Then reopen Fitness V3 and use Settings → Preview voice.

The exercise videos stay muted so they do not conflict with coaching. Speech is cancelled when the workout is paused, skipped, closed or sent to the background.

INSTALLATION
------------
1. Extract the ZIP.
2. Upload everything inside the extracted folder to the root of a GitHub repository.
3. Confirm index.html is visible on the repository’s main page.
4. Enable GitHub Pages: Settings → Pages → Deploy from a branch → main → /(root).
5. Open the Pages URL in iPhone Safari.
6. Share → Add to Home Screen.
7. Open it online once so the offline shell can be installed.

SAFETY
------
Fitness V3 provides general educational guidance, not diagnosis, rehabilitation or individual medical treatment. Start gradually. Stop for chest pain, faintness, severe or unusual breathlessness, or sharp/worsening pain. Seek appropriate professional advice when a medical condition, injury, pregnancy, recent surgery or prescribed restriction could affect exercise.

The meal section is general vegetarian guidance, not a prescribed calorie or therapeutic diet. Vegan users should pay particular attention to reliable vitamin B12 sources and seek professional advice where needed.
